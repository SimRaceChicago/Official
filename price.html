Open racing

Monday through Thursdays we host race nights where you can experience the thrill of driving for four hours straight, from 6 pm to 10 pm, for just $50. To ensure that everyone gets enough track time, we limit the number of drivers to 20 per night. This way, we can divide the participants into groups that alternate every half-hour.However, if we have less than 10 drivers on a particular race night, we will not impose any time limit, and you can enjoy driving as long as you want during the four-hour window. To reserve your spot, simply use the payment link that we will send you upon making a reservation.

Private event

Our private events commence at $800 on weekdays from Monday to Thursday, and $1000 on weekends from Friday to Sunday. When you book during these times, your party of 10 will receive three hours of racing time.As part of your booking, we are pleased to offer you the opportunity to invite up to 20 spectators to cheer on your party of 10. To ensure everyone stays refreshed, we will also provide complimentary water and soft drinks for the duration of your event.There are some upgradable options for an additional charge.

  • $200 - Extending the race time from three hours to four hours.
  • $200 - Add 10 more racers to make a total of 20 racers.
  • $400 - Photo booth for your party
  • $600 - 360 Photo booth for your party
  • $400 - A live steam of the event will be recorded and can be sent to everyone at the event and those who can't attend. This stream will include footage of the races including live comentary.
  • We are always here to assist you with any additional accommodations you may require. Please do not hesitate to contact us for further assistance.
